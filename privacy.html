Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2026

Inbox Puzzles respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect and how it is used.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide our service, which may include:

  • Your email address (for puzzle delivery and subscription management)
  • Basic subscription information

We do not collect sensitive personal information.

2. Puzzle Progress Storage

Some puzzles save progress locally in your browser using local storage. This information:

  • Is stored only on your device
  • Is not transmitted to our servers
  • Can be cleared at any time using the puzzle’s reset or clear option

3. Cookies

We may use limited cookies necessary for basic site functionality. We do not use cookies for advertising or behavioral tracking.

4. How We Use Information

Your information is used only to:

  • Deliver puzzle emails
  • Manage subscriptions
  • Improve the user experience

We do not sell or rent personal information.

5. Third-Party Services

Inbox Puzzles may rely on trusted third-party services for:

  • Email delivery
  • Website hosting

These services process data only as needed to provide their functionality and are subject to their own privacy policies.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

7. Your Choices

You may:

  • Unsubscribe from emails at any time
  • Request removal of your email address from our system

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Updates will be posted on this page.
